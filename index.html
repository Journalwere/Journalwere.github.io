About Our Club

The De Anza Computational Neuroscience Club is dedicated to exploring the fascinating intersection of neuroscience and computational techniques. Join us on a journey to understand the intricacies of the human mind through algorithms, simulations, and collaborative research.

Our Future Plans

As we look ahead, our club envisions organizing various events, workshops, and collaborative projects to delve deeper into the realms of computational neuroscience. Stay tuned for exciting activities such as hands-on coding sessions, guest lectures from industry experts, and opportunities to contribute to cutting-edge research in this field.
